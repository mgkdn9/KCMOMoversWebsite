Get to Know KCMO Movers

  Our Story


Hi, my name is Mike Kohlberg. I am the founder of KCMO Movers, and, believe it or not, I thoroughly enjoy moving people. After training for 7 months at the biggest branch of the biggest moving company in the US and completing an entrepreneurism internship, we broke ground in December 2014. We started moving under a different name and in a different city, but the goal was always the same: to provide quality moving services at an unbeatable price. This was possible given the lack of overhead that I knew we would have compared to some of the other providers. Years later, our level of integrity and precision has not changed. We enjoy raving reviews from residents and businesses of Kansas City, and we look forward to helping you take that next step in getting where you want to go!
